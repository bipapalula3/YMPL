The Birth of a Super Unit
GOT the beat consists of seven members spanning generations: K-pop icon BoA, Girls' Generation's Taeyeon and Hyoyeon, Red Velvet's Seulgi and Wendy, and aespa's Karina and Winter. They debuted in January 2022 with the digital single 'Step Back,' showcasing an overwhelming presence. This unit marked the beginning of the 'Girls On Top' project and garnered significant attention as a collaboration of SM female artists.
