Group Genesis & Special Meaning
GOT the beat is a project group that brings together top female artists from multiple generations under SM Entertainment, including BoA, Girls' Generation's Taeyeon and Hyoyeon, Red Velvet's Seulgi and Wendy, and aespa's Karina and Winter. Their unique presence and skills, honed in their respective groups, converge to create explosive synergy.
