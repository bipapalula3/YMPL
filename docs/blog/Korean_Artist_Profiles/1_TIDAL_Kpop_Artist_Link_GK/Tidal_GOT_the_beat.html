The Gathering of K-POP Empresses
GOT the beat is the first unit from SM Entertainment's 'Girls On Top (GOT)' project, launched in January 2022. It comprises seven artists representing different generations and groups: BoA, Taeyeon and Hyoyeon from Girls' Generation, Seulgi and Wendy from Red Velvet, and Karina and Winter from aespa. This dream team, showcasing the pinnacle of K-POP, generated immense anticipation among fans.

Solid Footprints and Special Stages
