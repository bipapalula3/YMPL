The Beginning and Expansion of His Musical Journey
Kang Seung won has primarily pursued independent musical activities rather than being part of a specific group, but his musical journey began in the 80s. He played a crucial role during the golden age of Korean popular music as one of the top session guitarists of his time. He later expanded his scope into composing and producing, broadening the horizons of Korean music through collaborations with various artists.

His Footprints in the Korean Music Scene
He has released numerous masterpieces, including his solo album 'Kang Seung won 1st Album', gaining recognition from both the public and critics.
