The Beginning of a Musical Journey, and Special Sensibility
Kang Seung won began building his profound musical world from the 1990s. His lyrical yet experimental music has inspired many artists.

The Presence of a Music Director Shining Behind the Stage

He is an unsung hero who elevated the standard of Korean music from behind the scenes.
