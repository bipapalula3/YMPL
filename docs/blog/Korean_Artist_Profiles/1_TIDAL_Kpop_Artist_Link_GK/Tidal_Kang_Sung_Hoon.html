Sechs Kies, The Dawn of an Era
Kang Sung Hoon made a stellar debut as the main vocalist of the six-member boy group Sechs Kies in 1997. They released numerous hit songs like 'School Byeolgok' and 'Pom Saeng Pom Sa,' leading the golden age of first-generation idols alongside their rival groups. His clear voice played a crucial role in completing the group's signature sound.

Solo Activities, Reunion, and New Challenges
After Sechs Kies disbanded in 2000, Kang Sung Hoon transitioned to a solo artist, continuing his activities with songs like 'My Girl' and 'My Hidden Story.' Later, in 2016, Sechs Kies made a dramatic reunion through 'Infinite Challenge,' receiving immense love from the public once again. Alongside group activities, he consistently showed his musical passion by communicating with fans.
